About Me - Jacob Heasley

Hello!
My name is Jacob Heasley.

I am a cybersecurity and forensic science student at Purdue University.

I am seeking employment as a digital forensic analyst.

I was born and raised in Iowa where I attended a community college for Computer Programming. In 2015, I married my high school sweetheart when she joined the U.S. Navy. I followed my spouse to Hawaii where we spent a year and a half on the island of Oʻahu. After Hawaii, we moved to Norfolk, Virginia where I graduated from Tidewater Community College. We now live in Indiana where I am attending Purdue University to major in Cybersecurity and minor in Forensic Sciences.

My interests include digital forensics, web design, and crime scene forensics.

I also love board games and listening to podcasts. My favorite podcast is Darknet Diaries by Jack Rhysider.
